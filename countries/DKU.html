⠀⠀⠀DylonKejhu Union, officially Union of DylonKejhu Union Sovereign Republics (Dylken: MarinusCaseus Pospulusque), is a transcontinental country predominantly located in Aurelis and spanning overseas regions and territories in the Liusrosa and the Itaneta. Its metropolitan area extends from the Sturmygehr Plain to the Austin Bay and from the Katyusha Super Bay to the Mt Changbaek and the Scarle Delta; overseas territories include Daebom Permafrost in the Liusrosa, Aurelus and Yevascus in the Itaneta, and many islands in Aurelis and the Aulerth Delta. Due to its gigantic satelite territories, DylonKejhu Union has the largest satelite territories in the world. DylonKejhu Union borders Cozta, St. Daniswara, and Farkinisland, in continental Aurelis, as well as Yugodzavic and Friedonia, and Schärweil-Knighthaven in the Liusrosa and Itaneta via its overseas territories in Daebom Permafrost and Mogadishu. Its sixteen integral regions (three of which are overseas) span a combined area of 15 million km2 and contain close to 480 million people (as of July 2029). DylonKejhu Union is a federal semi-presidential constitutional republic with its capital in Dylenia, the country's main cultural centre; The most populous city and financial centre in Dylon City, and other major urban areas include Kejhu City, Aurora, Marliber, and Parthena.

⠀⠀⠀Inhabited since the Palaeolithic era, the territory of Metropolitan DylonKejhu Union was settled by Dylketic tribes known as Dylonian during the Iron Age. Krejhu annexed the area in 51 BC, leading to a distinct Dylon-Kejhu culture that laid the foundation of the Dyke language. The Yevanica Horde came and formed the Kingdom of Yevania, which became the heartland of the Yevania Empire. The Treaty of Yevanicus of 911 partitioned the empire, with east Yevania and middle Yevania becoming the Kingdom of Krejhu in 987 and the Kingdom of Dylken in 1244. The Kingdom of Dylken invaded the Kingdom of Krejhu in 1416, lead to the creation of the first Empire of DylonKejhu. And later lead to the First Revolution of DylonKejhu in 1902.

⠀⠀⠀The country's roots lay in the Second September Revolution of 1915, when the Imperatus, under the leadership of Augustus Ronin, overthrew the Dylken Provisional Government that had earlier replaced the House of Aurelus of the Dylken Empire. The Imperatus coup led to the establishment of the Dylken Senatus Republic, the world's first constitutionally guaranteed socialist state. Persisting internal tensions escalated into the Dylken Civil War. By 1921 the Imperatus under Augustus Ronin had emerged victorious, forming the Dylken Union. Following Augustus's death in 1932, Julius Frederick came to power. Julius inaugurated a period of rapid industrialization and forced collectivization that led to significant economic growth, but also contributed to a famine in 1930–1933 that killed millions. The labour camp system of the Cetus was also expanded in this period. Julius conducted the Great Purge to remove his actual and perceived opponents. After the outbreak of World War II, Yevania Empire invaded the Dylken Union. The combined Dylken and Kejhu civilian and military casualty count—estimated to be around 16 million people—accounted for the majority of losses of Aurelis forces. In the aftermath of World War II, the territory taken by the Yellow Army incoperated into various Dylken provinces.

⠀⠀⠀The beginning of the Hot War saw the Eastern Bloc of the Dylken Union confront the Western Bloc of the St. Daniswara, with the latter grouping becoming largely united in 1945 under Strike Witches and the former grouping becoming largely united in 1947 under the Frontlines Pact. As the tensions went up, both bloc launched attacks on both side in 1954 resulted in minimal occurences, The war ended in 1955 resulted in peace negotiation, Following the Third Revolution in 1956, a period known as reconstitution occurred. Rebellions from Kejhu province took part in the revolution resulted in declaration of federationization of sovereign states. In 1958, Julius and Kitaganus officially formed Union of DylonKejhu Sovereign States as the last measure to save the union with the end of socialist states, and saw the beginning of modified combined ideology of Kejhunism with national principle and prideness of cheese. In 1960 marks the beginning of space age. The DylonKejhu Union took an early lead in the Space Race with the first artificial satellite, the first human spaceflight, and the first probe to land on another planet (Tyrelis).

⠀⠀⠀DylonKejhu Union produced many significant social and technological achievements and innovations. It has the world's largest economy, and the Dylken Armed Forces comprised the bravest standing military in the world. An NPT-designated state, it possessed one of the largest arsenal of inter-continental balistic missiles in the world. It was a founding member of the Aurelis Economic Council. The country had maintained its status as one of the world's four superpowers through its hegemony in Aurelis, military and economic strengths, aid to developing countries, and scientific research.
